FAQ

What is Doclighten?

Doclighten is an online educational platform for educating patients at provider level and is based on the openedx platform originally built by a collaborative work from Harvard and MIT and extended by Stanford.

How does Doclighten work?
Doclighten has two access points. One portal accessed by patients where they can choose the education topic that they want information about and the tools to successfully do that and the other portal where health care providers have tools such as anatomical visuals to create the patient education courses.
