FAQ

What's different about Plazapage?

Plazapage is socializing with other small business owners to make you, the small business owner, more productive. The focus is to access information that is crucial and credible to your business by socializing with your shopping center's other business owners.

What are the features?
All the features of a social website such as community Events Calendar, Discussion Forums, Members Directory, Private Messaging, Photo Albums and News. The productive side has a Business Front page for each business in the shopping center, Critical Contacts either for private or public(other business owners) use, Rating and Referral of services that you use such as merchant accounts, telephone systems, POS etc.
